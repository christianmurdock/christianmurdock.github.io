FYX Brand Identity

Summary

FYX is a fake water supplement company. This logo was done for a competition I participated in for SkillsUSA.

It was done for their national Advertising Design section, in which I receoved 3rd place out of 39 entries on the nations stage.

Overview

The prompt for this brand was a fake company that produces a water supplement containing creatine, caffiene, and all essential vitamins with three squirts. With this, I wanted to convey as much energy as possible, while also being bold and strong. I took inspiration from companies like Ghost Energy and Gorilla Mode.

Color Scheme
